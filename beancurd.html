Pandan Beancurd with Boba

Preparation Time: 40 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Pandan leaves - 7 leaves
  • Soy milk - 1 liter
  • Agar-agar powder - 5g
  • Water - 200ml
  • Pandan juice - 250ml
  • Green coloring - 2 drops (optional)
  • Frozen Boba
  • Pandan leaves - 2 leaves
  • Ginger - 3 slices
  • Brown sugar - 100g
  • Water - 200ml

Instructions

  1. Wash all pandan leaves and cut off the bottom part
  2. Cut 5 pandan leaves into smaller pieces into blender, add in 350 ml of water, then blend it. You may use 250 ml of water instead if you want heavier pandan taste
  3. Use a sift to remove the pandan leaves and leave it back with the pandan juice
  4. Add 200 ml of water into pot, add in 2 pandan tied leaves. Then add in 5g agar-agar powder. Boil it in medium low heat, and slowly stir it until it's boiling.
  5. Add in 1 liter of soy milk then add in pandan juice. Boil it with medium heat. Add in 2 drops of green food coloring which is optional but will make it prettier. Stir it well until it's boiling
  6. Remove the pandan leaves and then pour it into a container through a sift. Fridge for 2-3 hours until harden.
  7. Put brown sugar into a sauce pan, then heat it up until sugar melts
  8. Add in 200ml of water, ginger, and pandan leaves
  9. Boil it for 3 minutes.
  10. Cool it in the fridge.
  11. Heat boba in boiling water for 30 minutes, turn off the heat and stew for another 30 minutes with the lid on. Sieve and marinate with brown sugar syrup
  12. Add boba to pandan beancurd and serve
